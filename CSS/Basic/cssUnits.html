Two type of Units Possible

1) Absolute

  1. cm
  2. mm
  3. in
  4. px
  5. pt
  6. pc

2) Relative

  1. Em (Relative to Parent property)
  2. Rem (Relative to root(html tag)
  3. Vh (1% of view port height)
  4. Vw (1% of view port width)
  5. % (related to parent)
